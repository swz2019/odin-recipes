Toast Recipe

Buttered Toast

Toast is a breakfast staple around the country and an essential beginner recipe. The ingredients and steps are simple and easy to follow.

The trick for good toast is the timing, you never want to burn you bread!

Ingredients
  • Bread
  • Butter
Steps
  1. Put bread in toaster
  2. Set your toaster to 2.5min
  3. Remove and butter after timer
